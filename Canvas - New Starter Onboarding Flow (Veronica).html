ONBOARDING FLOW

Veronica's first ninety days, timed and templated.

The Position Description, Welcome Book and probation review pack already hold the substance. This is the spine underneath them: when each touchpoint fires, who sends it, and the exact words to send.

HOW THIS WORKS

One flow, the same shape every hire.

Nine triggers, from the day the contract is countersigned through to Day 90. Each trigger has a fixed timing, a named sender, and a template below it. The dates change with every new starter. The words do not.

Timing is relative to Veronica's actual start date so the flow can be set up once, in whatever system sends it (a calendar reminder, a Gmail template, or a workflow), and reused for the next hire without rebuilding it.

Nine triggers, Day 90.

03 · WHAT THIS ASSUMES

Fill before this goes live.

Veronica's confirmed start date · her exact start time and dress code · the Director's name for signature lines · the weekly 1:1 day and time · who meets her at the door on Day 1.

Once those are set, T6's three sends and the Day 90 confirmation date fall out automatically as T-3, T-3, T-3 and Day 90 from the start date. No other document changes.
